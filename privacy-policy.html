Privacy Policy

Welcome to Rezmytour! Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our travel services or access our website. By using Rezmytour, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Collection of Information

Rezmytour collects personal information to enhance your travel planning experience. This includes your name, email address, phone number, travel preferences, and payment details. Information is collected when you book a tour, sign up for our services, or subscribe to our newsletters.

Use of Information

We use your information to process bookings, personalize your travel recommendations, improve our services, and communicate special offers or updates. Your data helps us ensure a seamless and enjoyable experience for your trips.

Cookies

Cookies are used to improve your experience on our website. They help us understand your preferences, save your selections, and provide tailored recommendations. Cookies do not contain personal data and can be managed through your browser settings.

Sharing of Information

Rezmytour values your privacy and does not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to facilitate your bookings with travel service providers such as airlines, hotels, or tour operators.

Security Precautions

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access. All payment transactions are processed through secure gateways to ensure your financial information is safe.

Permissions

Rezmytour may request access to your device's location, camera, or contacts to provide better services, such as suggesting nearby attractions, enabling easy document uploads, or facilitating group bookings.

Choice/Opt-Out

You can opt out of receiving promotional communications from Rezmytour at any time. Simply follow the instructions in our emails or contact us directly.

Changes to Policy

Rezmytour may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please contact us at support@rezmytour.com. We’re here to help!
